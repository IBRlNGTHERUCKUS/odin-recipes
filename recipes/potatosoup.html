Perfect Potato Soup!

An Irish delight!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bacon, chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 cups chicken stock, or enough to cover potatoes
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried tarragon
  • 3 teaspoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook bacon, drain grease into pot.
  2. Cook celery and onion in the grease for 5 minutes. Stir in garlic and cook 2 more minutes. Finally, add potatos and cook 4 more minutes.
  3. Melt butter in seperate skillet and whisk in flower, heavy cream, tarragon, and cilantro. Cook 5 minutes.
  4. Blend and puree half the soup, then put in oven.
  5. Enjoy!
