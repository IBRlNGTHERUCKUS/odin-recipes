The Best Chocolate Chip Cookie Ever!

The perfect blend of salty and sweet

Ingredients

Dry:

  • 200 g all-purpose flour
  • 4 g Diamond Crystal
  • 4 g baking soda

Wet:

  • 169 g unsalted butter
  • 200 g (packed) dark brown sugar
  • 50 g granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 large egg yolk
  • 2 tsp. vanilla extract

Other:

  • 170 g semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 tbsp. himalayan salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the over to 350 F
  2. Add the dry ingredients into one bowl
  3. Melt and brown the butter
  4. Add the wet ingredients into another bowl
  5. Mix the wet and dry ingredients
  6. Add the chocolate chips
  7. Make small dough balls and put on baking tray
  8. Bake in over for 8-10 minutes
  9. Sprinkle the himalayan salt over them
  10. Enjoy!
